TMJC is one of the three junior colleges to offer the Drama Elective Programme. The Performance Lab is both a space for theatre experimentation and a flexible performance venue that can seat up to 120 audience members. Students offering H2 Theatre Studies and Drama attend their praxis seminars in the Lab to explore performance skills and techniques. Equipped with wireless theatrical lighting controls and sound systems, the comprehensive technical support brings out the best in the performance of the students.
