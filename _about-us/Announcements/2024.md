<h3>Nominations for the Most Inspiring Tamil Teacher Award (MITT) 2024 is now opened!</h3>
<p>The MITT Award is jointly presented by Tamil Murasu, Tamil Language Learning
and Promotion Committee (TLLPC), and the Singapore Tamil Teachers’ Union
(STTU).
<br>
<br>The award is to recognise teachers who have made significant contributions
to the teaching and learning of Tamil (TL).
<br>
<br>Nominees should be:</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Teachers who nurture students’ interest in TL by using innovative methods
to make lessons more interesting and engaging; and</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Dedicated and passionate teachers who demonstrate a keen interest in students’
well-being and inspire students to be self-directed and engaged learners.
<br>
</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p>Nomination Process
<br>Nominations can be made online at <a href="https://go.gov.sg/mitt2024nominationform" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">https://go.gov.sg/mitt2024nominationform</a>
<br>Do note that the online nomination closes on 1 April 2024.</p>

<h3>Nominations of the Arif Budiman Malay Language Teacher Award 2024 is now open!</h3>
<p><strong>The Arif Budiman Malay Language Teacher Award (AGAB) 2024</strong> is
jointly organised by the Malay Language Council, Singapore (MLCS), Malay
Language Teachers' Association (PGBM), Berita Harian (BH) and the Malay
Language Learning and Promotion Committee (MLLPC).</p>
<p>The annual award aims to recognise outstanding Malay Language teachers
who have made significant contributions to the teaching and learning of
Malay Language in our local mainstream schools.
<br>
<br>This year, awards will be given out under the following categories:
<br>Motivation (less than 10 years in teaching service); and
<br>Role Model (10 years and above in teaching service).
<br>Nominees will be evaluated based on</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>their ability to instill knowledge, skills and interest in the learning
of the Malay language, literature and culture, as well as their care and
concern for the well-being of their students.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p>Nominees must also demonstrate the values of Arif Budiman, a learned person
who contributes to society through excellent and exemplary service as an
educator.</p>
<p><strong>Nomination Process</strong> 
<br>Nominations can be made online at <a href="https://go.gov.sg/agab2024" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">https://go.gov.sg/agab2024</a>.
<br>Online Nomination closes on 1 April 2024.</p>
